About Me

I’m a 17-year-old UI/UX designer from Stuttgart, Germany, with a passion for creating designs that are clean, functional, and user-friendly. Right now, I’m focused on a project of mine, Lumina, which is all about optimizing workflows and helping people stay on top of their goals. I’m always looking to learn new things, whether it’s exploring the latest design trends, coding, or diving into new technologies. For me, design is more than just making things look good – it’s about solving problems and making experiences easier for people. I love taking on new challenges and pushing myself to grow, both as a designer and as a person.
